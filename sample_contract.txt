EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
The Employee shall serve as Software Developer and shall perform all duties assigned by the Company. The Employee shall work 40 hours per week and report to the Engineering Manager.

2. COMPENSATION
The Employee shall receive a salary of $75,000 per year, payable in bi-weekly installments. The Employee may be eligible for a performance bonus of up to 10% based on annual review.

3. TERM OF EMPLOYMENT
This agreement shall commence on January 15, 2024 and continue until terminated by either party. Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all Company information and trade secrets. This obligation shall survive termination of employment for a period of 2 years.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work products, inventions, and intellectual property created by the Employee during employment shall be the sole property of the Company.

6. NON-COMPETE
The Employee agrees not to work for a competing company within 50 miles of the Company's location for a period of 1 year after termination.

7. BENEFITS
The Employee shall be eligible for health insurance, dental coverage, and 401(k) retirement plan. The Company shall contribute 3% to the 401(k) plan.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
